Project Name: AI Gaming Growth Assistant

Overview:
An AI-powered YouTube growth assistant designed to help gaming creators increase views, improve click-through rate (CTR), and optimize content strategy using advanced prompt engineering.

Problem:
Most small gaming creators struggle with:

Low CTR due to weak titles and thumbnails
Poor retention due to slow or boring hooks
Lack of viral content ideas
No clear growth strategy

Solution:
Developed a custom AI assistant that provides:

Viral video ideas based on proven formats (challenge, curiosity, high-stakes)
High-CTR title generation with ranked variations
Thumbnail strategy with clear visual concepts
Step-by-step growth plans tailored to channel size and niche
Shorts strategy for faster reach and discovery

Key Features:

Structured output system (Ideas → Strategy → Execution → Pro Tips)
Indian audience optimization (Hinglish + relatable formats)
Performance benchmarks (CTR %, retention targets)
Content system approach (idea → title → script → distribution)

Tech Stack:

Prompt Engineering
ChatGPT Custom GPT

Use Cases:

Gaming YouTubers (0–10K subs)
Content creators looking to improve video performance
Freelancers offering YouTube growth services

Results:

Faster content creation workflow
Improved video packaging (titles + thumbnails)
Better audience engagement and retention potential

Future Scope:

Integration with API-based automation tools
Expansion to multi-niche content (tech, education, finance)
Advanced analytics-based recommendations

Demo:
Includes real use-case outputs such as:

Viral GTA 5 video ideas
Title rewrites for low-performing videos
Growth strategy breakdown for small channels
